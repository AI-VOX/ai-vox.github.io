## What is AI-VOX?

AI-VOX is a curated collection of demonstrations, tutorials, and best practices for building intelligent enterprise solutions using:

- **Agentic AI** - Autonomous AI agents that can reason, plan, and execute tasks
- **SAP Integration** - AI-powered automation for SAP systems
- **ERP Automation** - Streamlining enterprise resource planning with AI
- **Video Showcases** - Real-world demos and walkthroughs
